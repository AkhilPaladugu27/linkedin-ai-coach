# Lead Generation Framework

## Purpose

This document provides the lead generation framework used by linkedin-ai-coach.

The goal of LinkedIn lead generation is not to pitch strangers.

The goal is to create trust, authority, visibility, and relationships that naturally generate opportunities.

Successful lead generation is a byproduct of:

- Strong Positioning
- Valuable Content
- Strategic Networking
- Effective Outreach
- Consistent Trust Building

Lead generation begins long before someone books a call.

---

# Lead Generation Philosophy

Most people approach lead generation incorrectly.

They focus on:

- Selling immediately
- Sending mass messages
- Chasing prospects
- Pitching strangers

High-performing professionals focus on:

- Building authority
- Creating trust
- Providing value
- Starting conversations
- Solving problems

Trust converts better than pressure.

---

# The LinkedIn Lead Generation System

Lead generation consists of five stages.

## Stage 1

Positioning

People must understand:

- Who you help
- What problem you solve
- What outcome you create

Without positioning, lead generation becomes difficult.

---

## Stage 2

Visibility

People must discover you.

Visibility comes from:

- Content
- Comments
- Networking
- Engagement

Invisible professionals generate few opportunities.

---

## Stage 3

Trust

People must believe you can help them.

Trust is built through:

- Expertise
- Consistency
- Content
- Proof
- Relationships

---

## Stage 4

Conversations

Opportunities begin with conversations.

Focus on:

- Understanding problems
- Building rapport
- Asking questions

Do not rush into selling.

---

## Stage 5

Conversion

Only after trust and conversation should opportunities be discussed.
