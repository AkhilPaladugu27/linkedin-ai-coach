# Content Creation Framework

## Purpose

This document provides the content creation system used by linkedin-ai-coach.

The goal of content creation is not posting more.

The goal is creating content that:

- Builds Authority
- Creates Trust
- Generates Engagement
- Starts Conversations
- Attracts Opportunities
- Strengthens Positioning

Every post should provide value.

Every post should have a purpose.

---

# The Content Creation Philosophy

Most content fails because it focuses on attention.

Successful content focuses on value.

The best LinkedIn content:

- Solves problems
- Shares experiences
- Teaches lessons
- Challenges assumptions
- Creates conversations

Always prioritize usefulness over popularity.

---

Hook

# The Teaching Framework

Do not simply share outcomes.

Teach the process.

Instead of:

"We generated 500 leads."

Use:

"Here is the process that helped generate 500 leads."

Teaching builds authority.

---

# Formatting Framework

LinkedIn content should be easy to consume.

Use:

- Short paragraphs
- Line breaks
- Bullet points
- Numbered lists

Avoid:

- Large text blocks
- Complex language
- Long paragraphs

Clarity wins.

---

# CTA Framework

Every post does not need a CTA.

When used, CTAs should encourage discussion.

---

## Experience CTA

Have you experienced something similar?

---

## Opinion CTA

What's your perspective?

---

## Reflection CTA

What's the biggest lesson you've learned?

---

## Application CTA

How would you apply this?

---

## Discussion CTA

Do you agree or disagree?

---

Avoid:

- Comment YES
- Engagement bait
- Artificial interaction requests

# Content Quality Checklist

Before publishing ask:

Does this provide value?

Does this teach something?

Does this align with my positioning?

Does this help my audience?

Does this build trust?

Would I stop scrolling to read this?

If not, improve the content.
