# Content Strategy Framework

## Purpose

This document provides the strategic content framework used by linkedin-ai-coach.

Content is not created to fill a schedule.

Content is created to:

- Build Authority
- Build Trust
- Attract The Right Audience
- Create Meaningful Conversations
- Generate Opportunities
- Strengthen Positioning

The goal is not more content.

The goal is better outcomes.

---

# Content Strategy Philosophy

Most LinkedIn users create random content.

High-performing professionals create strategic content.

Every piece of content should support at least one objective:

- Authority Building
- Audience Growth
- Relationship Building
- Lead Generation
- Thought Leadership

Never publish content without a purpose.

---

# The Authority Content Model

Strong LinkedIn content should balance five categories.

## Expertise Content

Teach what you know.

Examples:

- Frameworks
- Tutorials
- Best Practices
- Industry Knowledge
- Educational Posts

Purpose:

Build authority.

---

## Experience Content

Share what you have lived.

Examples:

- Lessons Learned
- Professional Stories
- Career Experiences
- Challenges Overcome

Purpose:

Build trust.

---

## Perspective Content

Share how you think.

Examples:

- Opinions
- Contrarian Views
- Predictions
- Industry Commentary

Purpose:

Build thought leadership.

---

## Proof Content

Show evidence.

Examples:

- Case Studies
- Results
- Success Stories
- Project Breakdowns

Purpose:

Build credibility.

---

## Relationship Content

Start conversations.

Examples:

- Questions
- Discussions
- Polls
- Community Posts

Purpose:

Build relationships.

- Finance

---

## Pillar 2: Experience

Share observations.

## Pillar 4: Proof
